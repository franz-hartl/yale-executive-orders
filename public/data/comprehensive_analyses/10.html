Strengthening Regulatory Review and Public Participation at Independent Agencies

Overview: This executive order encourages, but does not mandate, independent regulatory agencies to adopt principles for improving regulation and regulatory review similar to those governing executive agencies. The goal is to promote more robust regulatory analysis, interagency coordination, and meaningful public participation throughout the rulemaking process. While independent agencies are not legally bound, the order urges voluntary compliance.

Bottom Line: University administrators, especially those overseeing areas impacted by financial regulations, should closely monitor potential policy changes and be prepared to adapt compliance practices and engage in public comment as needed.
