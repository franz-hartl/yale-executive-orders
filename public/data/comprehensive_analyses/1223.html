Executive Order on Expanding Access to In Vitro Fertilization and Reproductive Health Services

Overview: This executive order directs the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) to take actions supporting access to in vitro fertilization (IVF) and other reproductive health services. It aims to expand availability and affordability of assisted reproductive technologies, though specific initiatives are not yet defined.

Bottom Line: Limited immediate regulatory impact on higher education institutions, but potential long-term effects on employee health benefits and student services.

Builds on previous efforts to require insurance coverage for infertility treatments and ensure access to reproductive health services. Follows recent state actions restricting or protecting abortion.
