Prohibiting Transgender Athletes in Women's Sports

Overview: This executive order bans all transgender women from participating in women's athletics at the high school and college levels nationwide. It asserts that transgender women have unfair physiological advantages over cisgender women, undermining Title IX's goal of equal opportunity. Universities that allow transgender athletes to compete as women risk losing federal funding.

Bottom Line: To safeguard federal funds, universities must ban transgender women from women's sports despite significant legal and inclusion risks.
