Overview: Aimed at downsizing the federal government, this executive order requires all agencies to identify positions and functions that can be eliminated. It imposes an immediate government-wide hiring freeze, with limited exceptions for essential roles. The order is likely to result in decreased federal funding and research support for universities.

Bottom Line: Universities must thoroughly evaluate their exposure to federal funding cuts and personnel losses, developing comprehensive contingency plans to maintain financial and operational stability.
