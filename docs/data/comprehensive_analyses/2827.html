Executive Order 14114: Ensuring Lawful Governance and Implementing Deregulatory Agenda

Overview: In this Executive Order, President Biden aims to implement his administration's deregulatory agenda by tasking federal agencies with reviewing significant regulations and identifying those that may be unlawful, ineffective, or unnecessarily burdensome. The stated goal is to ensure regulations are consistent with law, transparent and accessible, guided by scientific evidence, and minimally burdensome.

Bottom Line: Higher education institutions should closely monitor and prepare for potential significant changes to federal regulations across a range of administrative functions. Proactive planning and participation in rulemaking process will be important.
