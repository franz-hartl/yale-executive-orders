Financial System Regulation Review

Overview: The president ordered a review of financial regulations to align with core principles for regulating the U.S. financial system.

Key Impacts on Yale:

  • Potential changes to administrative compliance requirements for Yale's financial operations
  • Possible revisions to Yale's financial regulations and policies
  • Need to monitor for new or modified federal financial regulations applicable to the university

Action Needed: Yes

Important Dates: N/A

Affected Departments: Office of the President, Office of the Provost, Office of General Counsel, Office of Finance and Administration

What Changed

The president ordered a review of existing financial regulations to ensure they align with new core principles for regulating the U.S. financial system.

Why Yale Cares

As a large university with significant financial operations, Yale must comply with federal financial regulations. Any changes to these regulations could impact Yale's administrative processes and policies.

Action Steps

  • Monitor for updates on the review of financial regulations
  • Assess the potential impact of any regulatory changes on Yale's financial operations
  • Revise university policies and procedures as needed to maintain compliance

Resource Needs

Staff time to review new regulations, update policies, and implement any necessary changes
