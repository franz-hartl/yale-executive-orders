Restored Iran Sanctions for Universities

Overview: The U.S. government reimposed economic and financial sanctions on Iran that affect universities.

Key Impacts on Yale:

  • Restrictions on research and educational collaborations with Iranian partners
  • Limits on financial transactions and procurement related to Iran
  • Increased compliance requirements for Iran-related activities

Action Needed: Yes

Important Dates: 08/06/2018: Executive Order 13846 issued

Affected Departments: Office of Research Administration, Office of General Counsel, Procurement Services, International Affairs

What Changed

The U.S. government reinstated sanctions on Iran that were previously lifted under the Iran nuclear deal.

Why Yale Cares

Yale has research, educational, and financial ties to Iran that are now subject to new restrictions and compliance requirements.

Action Steps

  • Review all current and planned activities related to Iran
  • Implement processes to ensure compliance with the restored sanctions
  • Communicate the changes to relevant faculty, staff, and students

Resource Needs

Additional staff time and legal expertise to manage compliance
