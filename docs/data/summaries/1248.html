Ansar Allah Designated as Foreign Terrorist Organization

Overview: Executive Order 2025-02103 designates Ansar Allah (the Houthis) as a Foreign Terrorist Organization, imposing additional sanctions and restrictions on the group. Universities must review financial and operational ties to ensure compliance.

Bottom Line: Universities should immediately assess potential exposure and take steps to comply with new sanctions.

Key Impacts on Higher Education:

Research Collaborations: Review any research projects or collaborations involving Ansar Allah for potential sanctions violations.

Financial Transactions: Analyze financial ties and transactions to identify any funds or assets associated with the designated group.

Contracts and Agreements: Evaluate existing contracts and agreements to determine any exposure to Ansar Allah and the new sanctions.

Important Dates:

  • 01/31/2025: Designation effective date

Affected Departments:

Research Administration, Financial Affairs, Legal Counsel, International Programs

Action Plan:

Action Required: Yes. Universities must proactively assess and address potential exposure to remain compliant.

Immediate Steps (0-30 days):

  • Inform key stakeholders of the new designation and sanctions.
  • Initiate a comprehensive review of research, financial, and contractual relationships.
  • Consult with legal counsel on compliance requirements and potential risks.

Short-term Actions (1-3 months):

  • Develop and implement necessary changes to policies, procedures, and systems.
  • Communicate with affected faculty, staff, and students about the implications.

Long-term Considerations:

  • Monitor for any additional sanctions or guidance related to Ansar Allah.
  • Incorporate reviews into ongoing compliance and risk management processes.

Resource Requirements:

Personnel: May require additional staff time to conduct necessary reviews and implement changes.

Budget: Potential for some budget impact to adjust systems and support remediation efforts.

Technology: Likely minimal, potentially some updates to financial and research management systems.

External Expertise: Legal counsel guidance on specific compliance obligations and risks.
