Limits on Investing in Chinese Military Companies

Overview: The order prohibits investing in companies tied to the Chinese military.

Key Impacts on Yale:

  • Affects Yale's retirement and investment plans
  • Requires reviewing portfolio holdings
  • May impact research collaborations with Chinese entities

Action Needed: Yes

Important Dates: 11/12/2020: Executive Order issued

Affected Departments: Finance and Investments, Research Administration, Procurement

What Changed

The order prohibits U.S. persons from investing in publicly traded securities of companies identified as Communist Chinese military companies.

Why Yale Cares

Yale has retirement plans and other investments that may be affected. Some Yale research collaborations could also be impacted.

Action Steps

  • Review investment portfolio to identify any affected holdings
  • Develop plan to divest from prohibited investments by the deadline
  • Assess impact on research collaborations with Chinese entities

Resource Needs

Staff time to review investments and research partnerships, potential costs to divest from prohibited holdings
