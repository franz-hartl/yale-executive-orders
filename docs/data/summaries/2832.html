Improving Federal Service Delivery and Customer Experience

Overview: Directs federal agencies to enhance service delivery and customer experience to restore trust in government. Aims to make it easier for the public to access federal services and benefits.

Bottom Line: Universities should monitor how this order affects interactions with federal agencies, especially around research funding.

Key Impacts on Higher Education:

Research Grants: Application and reporting processes for federal research grants may be streamlined.

Data Sharing: Increased emphasis on data sharing between agencies could impact reporting requirements.

Public Engagement: Opportunities may arise to provide input on improving higher education-related federal services.

Important Dates:

  • 12/13/2021: Executive order issued

Affected Departments:

Sponsored Projects Office, Research Administration, Government Relations

Action Plan:

Action Required: No immediate action required, but relevant offices should track implementation progress.

Immediate Steps (0-30 days):

  • Review order and identify potential impact areas
  • Discuss implications with affected departments

Short-term Actions (1-3 months):

  • Monitor agency implementation plans
  • Engage with professional associations tracking this issue

Long-term Considerations:

  • Proactively provide input on customer experience improvements
  • Adapt processes to streamlined grant requirements

Resource Requirements:

Personnel: No additional personnel needed

Budget: No significant budget impact

Technology: May need to update grants management systems

External Expertise: Rely on sector associations for implementation details
